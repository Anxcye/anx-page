# Introduction

Anx Reader is a thoughtfully crafted e-book reader for book lovers. Featuring powerful AI capabilities and supporting various e-book formats, it makes reading smarter and more focused. With its modern interface design, we're committed to delivering pure reading pleasure.

## Key Features

*   **Powerful AI Capabilities**: Integrates with AI services to provide smart features like content summarization.
*   **Multi-Format Support**: Supports extensive e-book formats including EPUB, MOBI, TXT, ensuring compatibility with most e-books.
*   **Seamless Sync**: Access your reading progress, notes, and books across various platforms like iOS, Android, Windows, and Mac.
*   **Reading Analytics**: Track your reading habits with professional analytics, including detailed reports and an intuitive heat map.
*   **Customization**: Personalize your reading environment by adjusting themes, line spacing, margins, and font styles.

## Community Projects

The following projects are maintained by the community:
*   **Calibre Plugin**: [anx-reader-calibre-plugin](https://github.com/ptbsare/anx-reader-calibre-plugin) - Enables direct management of your Anx Reader ebook library from Calibre.
*   **Web Library Manager**: [anx-calibre-manager](https://github.com/ptbsare/anx-calibre-manager) - A modern web application for managing your ebook library with Calibre integration and WebDAV functionality.
